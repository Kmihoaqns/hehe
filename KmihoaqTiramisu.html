  • Tiramisu is a famous Italian dessert.
  • It consists of layers of smooth, sweet Savoiardi biscuits, creamy cheese, a hint of fragrant rum, and the lingering bitterness of coffee and cocoa.
  • These soft, fluffy biscuits are dipped into a rich espresso, creating a subtly bitter taste and an alluring aroma.
  • A classic Italian dessert, distinguished by layers of coffee and liqueur-infused ladyfingers, interspersed with rich mascarpone cream and egg, topped with bitter cocoa powder, creating a harmonious blend of sweet, slightly bitter, creamy, and aromatic flavors.
